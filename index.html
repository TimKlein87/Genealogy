Welcome to my genealogy website.

Hi! My name is Tim Klein, and I've been a hobby-genealogist for a couple of years now. I've created this website to collaborate and share information with other people. Did you find this website and have anything to add or any questions? Feel free to contact me! (contact via Facebook).

My Genealogies

Genealogy Crosta
I'm related to the Crosta branch through my 2nd great-grandmother Anna Theresia Mathilda Crosta (1862). The branch started in the Netherlands when Pietro Crosta (1791) moved from Germasino, Italy to Amsterdam. I'm currently in contact with the website administrator of www.crosta.it to find out more about the history of this branch.

My research

All my research is available on ancestry.com. Because that's a paid website, I publish everything to www.genealogieonline.nl as well.

What is github and why I chosen it

Github is a tool for collaborating and distributing (mostly software projects). However it can also be used to collaborate on this website, it allows easy editing of the information and it takes care of the hosting of the website.
